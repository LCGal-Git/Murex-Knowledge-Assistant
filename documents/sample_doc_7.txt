MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - STALE FX MARKET DATA FEED

Issue observed (2025-05-12): FX spot rates displayed in the trading
blotter for USD/MXN were not updating in real time, showing rates
roughly 40 minutes old, which caused several trades to be booked at
off-market rates before the issue was noticed.

Root cause: The market data feed connection to the primary data vendor
had silently disconnected due to an expired authentication certificate,
but Murex continued displaying the last successfully received rate
without visibly flagging it as stale.

Resolution: Renewed the market data vendor's authentication certificate
and restarted the market data gateway service. Added a monitoring alert
under Market Data > Feed Monitoring to flag any feed with no update for
longer than 2 minutes during trading hours.

Preventive note: Certificate expiry dates for market data vendor
connections should be tracked proactively, since a silent disconnection
is far more dangerous than an obvious error - traders may not notice
rates have stopped moving.
