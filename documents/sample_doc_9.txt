MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - SETTLEMENT NETTING NOT APPLYING CORRECTLY

Issue observed (2025-06-02): FX trades with counterparty NORDIC_FX_AB
that were expected to net into a single payment per currency per value
date were instead generating separate, unnetted settlement instructions,
increasing operational payment volume unnecessarily.

Root cause: The netting agreement flag on the counterparty's static
data record had been reset to "No Netting" during an unrelated static
data cleanup exercise performed by the onboarding team, without
awareness that a netting agreement was in place with this counterparty.

Resolution: Re-enabled netting under Back Office > Settlement >
Counterparty Netting Agreement, and confirmed with Legal that the
signed netting agreement was still valid. Reprocessed the current
day's trades so they netted correctly going forward.

Preventive note: Static data cleanup exercises should cross-check
netting agreement flags against a maintained list of legally signed
netting agreements before making bulk changes, since this flag is easy
to overwrite unintentionally.
