MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - END-OF-DAY ACCOUNTING BATCH FAILURE

Issue observed (2025-06-15): The end-of-day accounting simulation batch
for entity BANK_MX_01 failed partway through processing, leaving
roughly 200 trades without generated accounting entries for the day,
delaying the morning P&L report.

Root cause: A single trade in the batch had a null value in the
settlement date field due to a manual booking error, and the batch job
was not configured to skip and log individual failures - a single bad
trade halted the entire batch.

Resolution: Corrected the settlement date on the affected trade,
re-ran the accounting simulation batch overnight, and it completed
successfully for all 200 trades. Submitted a change request to
reconfigure the batch job to log and skip individual trade failures
rather than halting entirely.

Preventive note: Critical end-of-day batch jobs should be configured
with failure isolation (skip-and-log) rather than all-or-nothing
processing, so one bad trade doesn't block an entire day's accounting
output.
