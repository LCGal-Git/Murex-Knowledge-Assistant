MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - SETTLEMENT INSTRUCTION (SSI) MISMATCH

Issue observed (2025-03-10): Settlement instructions generated for FX
spot trades under entity BANK_MX_02 were routing payments to an
incorrect nostro account for USD settlements.

Root cause: Two SSI (Standard Settlement Instruction) records existed
for the same counterparty - one legacy record and one updated record
added after a correspondent bank change. The workflow was still
referencing the legacy SSI due to an incorrect priority flag.

Resolution: Deactivated the legacy SSI record under Back Office >
Settlement > SSI Management, and set the updated record as the sole
active instruction for USD. Reprocessed the three affected trades
manually to regenerate correct settlement messages.

Preventive note: When updating SSI records, always deactivate the
old record rather than leaving both active with priority flags, since
priority logic can be misread by downstream workflows.
