MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - PRE-TRADE CREDIT LIMIT BREACH

Issue observed (2025-01-22): Traders on the FX desk were unable to book
new forward trades with counterparty GLOBALBANK_LTD, receiving a hard
block from the pre-trade credit control module despite the counterparty
having available credit headroom according to Risk.

Root cause: The credit limit utilization calculation was including two
already-matured trades that had not been properly rolled off the
exposure calculation due to a batch job that failed silently the
previous night.

Resolution: Manually triggered a recalculation of credit utilization
under Risk > Pre-Deal Checks > Recalculate Exposure, which correctly
excluded the matured trades. Investigated the failed batch job and
found a lock conflict with a concurrent end-of-day process.

Preventive note: Pre-trade credit blocks that seem inconsistent with
Risk's own view of exposure should prompt a check of the most recent
exposure recalculation batch before assuming a limit is genuinely
exhausted.
