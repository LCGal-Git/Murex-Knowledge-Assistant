MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - POST-TRADE STP WORKFLOW STUCK IN PENDING

Issue observed (2025-02-18): A batch of IRS (interest rate swap) trades
booked by the Rates desk remained stuck in "Pending Validation" status
overnight instead of progressing automatically through the STP
(straight-through-processing) workflow to confirmation generation.

Root cause: A recently added post-trade validation rule (checking for
a mandatory clearing broker field on cleared trades) was misconfigured
to apply to both cleared AND uncleared trades. Since uncleared IRS
trades don't have a clearing broker, they failed the check and froze
in the workflow.

Resolution: Corrected the rule's scope under Post-Trade > Workflow
Rules > Validation Rules to apply only to trades flagged as cleared.
Manually released the stuck trades through the pending queue once the
rule was fixed.

Preventive note: Any new post-trade validation rule should be tested
against both cleared and uncleared trade populations before deployment,
since rules are easy to scope too broadly by default.
