MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - USER UNABLE TO APPROVE TRADES IN WORKFLOW

Issue observed (2025-04-20): A newly onboarded middle office analyst
reported being unable to approve trades pending in their queue, with
the approval button greyed out despite having been granted "Middle
Office Analyst" role in the user administration module.

Root cause: The role had been assigned correctly, but the user's
profile was missing an entity-level entitlement for BANK_MX_01, so
even though the role itself allowed approvals, the user had no
permission scope over trades booked under that specific entity.

Resolution: Added the missing entity entitlement under User
Administration > Entitlements > Entity Scope for the affected user.
Approval functionality worked immediately after entitlement was added,
without needing to log out or restart the session.

Preventive note: When onboarding new users, both the functional role
AND the entity-level entitlement need to be checked - granting a role
alone does not guarantee access if entity scope is missing.
