MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - MT940 STATEMENT RECONCILIATION BREAK

Issue observed (2025-04-05): Daily nostro reconciliation for the EUR
account at CUSTODIAN_BANK showed three unmatched entries after the
MT940 statement import, even though the underlying trades had settled
correctly according to Back Office.

Root cause: The MT940 parser was misreading a non-standard reference
format used by CUSTODIAN_BANK in field 86, causing the matching engine
to treat those three entries as unrecognized rather than linking them
to existing settlement records.

Resolution: Added a custom parsing rule under Back Office >
Reconciliation > Statement Formats to correctly extract the reference
number from CUSTODIAN_BANK's field 86 format. Reprocessed the
statement and all three entries matched automatically.

Preventive note: When onboarding a new custodian or nostro
correspondent, request a sample MT940 statement in advance to verify
field 86 reference formatting before go-live, rather than discovering
mismatches in production.
