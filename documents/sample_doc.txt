MUREX CONFIGURATION NOTE - FX FORWARD ACCOUNTING FLOW

Issue observed (2024-11-03): FX forward trades booked through the front
office were not flowing to the accounting ledger for entity BANK_MX_01.

Root cause: The accounting simulation model for FX forwards was missing
a mapping to the correct general ledger account group for this entity.
This mapping exists by default for entity BANK_MX_00 but was not copied
over during the entity setup for BANK_MX_01.

Resolution: Added the missing GL account group mapping under
Datamart > Accounting > Simulation Models > FX Forward, replicating the
configuration used for BANK_MX_00. Trades booked after the fix flowed
correctly to accounting within the next end-of-day batch.

Preventive note: When onboarding a new legal entity, always verify that
accounting simulation model mappings are copied from a reference entity,
not just static data and workflow rules.
